Place the ExampleArmorMaterial.java and ModArmor.java file into src/main/java/.../ it should be next to your ModItems file
You can rename the class ExampleArmorMaterial to the name of the Armor Material you wish to create.

Inside the file you can edit your armor's attributes. Follow the comments.

Add this line to your datagen/ModItemTagProvider to make your modded item capable of repairing your armor in an Anvil

        valueLookupBuilder(ExampleArmorMaterial.REPAIRS_EXAMPLE_ARMOR)
                .add(ModItems.EXAMPLE_ITEM);


There are two main textures for armor, the items themselves, and the appearance on the player's body
The item textures are in assets/.../textures/item
The way the armor looks on the player is in assets/.../textures/entity/equipment/humanoid and humanoid_leggings

Go into your ModArmor class and edit the variable names and datagen names
